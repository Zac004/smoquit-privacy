Data Collection & Privacy

SmoQuit is designed with privacy-by-design principles. All data is stored locally on your device only.

DATA WE STORE LOCALLY:

  • Timeline Information: Start date of your quit journey and timestamps of resistance/lapse events for progress calculation only.
  • Financial Preferences: Cigarette cost estimate and currency preference for savings calculations. No banking or payment information.
  • Notification Settings: Your chosen notification schedule and custom reminder times for local notification scheduling only.
